Astrophotography Courses

Learn the art of astrophotography with our comprehensive courses.

Beginner Course

Learn from scratch with our beginner-friendly course. No prior experience required.

Solar Photography Course

Learn how to safely photograph the sun and its features with a Hydrogen-Alpha and White light filter.

Planetary Photography Course

Photographs the planets using one of the best planetary telescopes available on the market. During oppositions of the planets, our 130 year old Clark telescope will be used for imaging.

Deep Space Photography Course

Take your experience a step further and use a true astrograph to image nebula, galaxies, and star clusters. Fundamental knowledge and some experience preferred.
